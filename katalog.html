LANGUAGE GROUP:
OUR CONCEPT


It is a playgroup that emphasizes learning the Spanish language. The language play group is aimed at toddlers from around 2.5 years up to compulsory school entry.

It is based on the educational goals set out in the Orientation Framework for Early Childhood Education, Care and Upbringing in Switzerland at www.orientierungsrahmen.ch and www.spielgruppe.ch.

Spanish is spoken in the playgroup. Playful activities are carried out to specifically promote the children's language skills.
