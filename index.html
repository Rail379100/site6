SOCIAL NETWORKS

GOAL AND VISION

In “Spanish for Children” your children will learn and enjoy Spanish from an early age. They will do a myriad of activities, games, and exercises, and interact with other children.

You will playfully immerse yourself in the world of the Spanish language in order to not only improve your linguistic, but also social, psychomotor and cognitive skills.

The Spanish language is taught in an inclusive and open way, so the different language accents, vocabularies and expressions of the Spanish-speaking countries of the world are welcome.
